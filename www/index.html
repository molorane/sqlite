About

The Sesotho Bible that I fondly call "Buka ea Khale", is an application developed with the sole purpose of serving the spritual needs of all people who can read and understand Sesotho language. The application is free of charge and it is illegal to sell it! This application may not give correct scripture references according to the canon of the scriptures. I believe as we continue finding incorrect scripture references and updating it,finally we shall have a perfect application with correct references. Owing to that, I urge anyone who come across such errors to report them to molorane.mothusi@gmail.com

Developer

Molorane Mothusi Michael

I created the sesotho bible in order to have access to the word of God in my native language. Initially, the bible was intended for an individual use.

However, the initial idea faded into the thin air, as the need and opportunity to serve the wider community became apparent. Now the application is available to everyone free of charge.

Sesotho Bible is now available in all platforms making it easier for people having devices that run operating systems such as ios,android,blackberry10 and windows phone to have access to the word of God.

God bless each and every one of you. I hope Buka Ea Khale will help you to have access to riches of Heaven, everywhere, anytime and all the time.
